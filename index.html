Characters in Noli Mi Tangere
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, or Crisostomo as he is commonly known, is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra.
  • He is a smart and well respected young man who is the main character in the story of Noli Mi Tangere
  • He studied in Spain for 7 years and lost all communication with his family and friends in the Philippines during his time there.
    		•
    Padre Bernardo Salví
  • Padre Bernardo Salvi, commonly known as Padre Salvi, is the current curate of the town of San Diego, replacing Padre Damaso before him.
  • Like many of the other priests in the story, he abuses his power and manipulates the town's people.
    • Narcisa
  • Narcisa, or Sisa as she is commonly known as, is a young beautiful woman who is the mother of Crispin and Basilio
  • She is abused by her husband, Pedro, but she cannot bring herself to leave him.
  • She sees her husband as her Lord and Savior and her children as angels.
    		•
    Don Santíago de los Santos
  • Captain Tiago, as he is common known as, is a rich Filipino business man who is also the father of Maria Clara.
  • Even if he is Filipino by blood, he is a Spaniard at heart as he does not see himself as a Filipino.
  • Captain Tiago however is a decent man as he is kind although fails to represent his country
    		•
    Don Anastacio
  • Don Anastacio, or Pilosopher Tasio as he is known among the town's people, is an old scholar who lives in San Diego.
  • He is an intelligent man who because of his intelligence, is called a philosopher or a mad man by the people.
  • Unlike the other people in the town, he does not believe in religion because he sees right through the lies that the priests preach and instead, he believes in rationality.
  • He is also very wise as the people who recognize his intelligence ask him for advice.
    • Dámaso Verdolagas
  • Dámaso Verdolagas, or Padre Damaso as is commonly known as, is a high ranking priest who served as the curate of San Diego for 20 years.
  • Despite having been in the Philippines for 20+ years, he has not bothered to learn the native language and treats Filipinos as if they are all stupid, mindless people.
  • He has let the power get to his head and has become very corrupt, narcissictic, and entitled.
    		•
    Isabel Alba
  • Tiya Isabel, or Tiya Isabel, is the Aunt of Maria Clara who helped her raised her.
  • She is the mother figure of Maria Clara as Maria Clara's mother passed away when she was young.
  • She is also the head housekeeper of the Kapitan Tiago's house.
    		•
    María Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • Maria Clara de los Santos y Alba, or just Maria Clara, is the daughter of Capitan Tiago.
  • She was known all around the town of San Diego for being a beautiful young woman.
  • Can be considered the female heroine of the entire novel.
  • Was bethrothed to Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Also studied in a convent for 7 years, while Ibarra was in Spain.
    		•
    Basilio
  • Is the son of Sisa and the older brother of Crispin.
  • Wanted to be an altar server however, he witnessed the corrupt and unfair treatment of the priests.
  • Ran back home and planned for him, Crispin, and his mother to go to Crisostomo Ibarra so that he may work for him.
    • Sibyla
  • Sibyla, or Padre Sibyla is a Catholic priest.
  • Is affiliated with Padre Damaso and loves the power that he has.
  • Just like Padre Damaso, he believes that the Filipinos are stupid people and is very much narcissistic.
  • He was seen giving and recieving empty praise to and from Padre Damaso so he could get the head seat at the table, showing the priest's lies and tactics of deception.
